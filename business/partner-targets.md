## Integration Roadmap

### Phase 1: Immediate (0-3 months)
1. **Microsoft 365**
   - **Rationale**: Widely used in remote environments, essential for IT departments.

   - **Affiliate/Revenue-share**: Yes, through Microsoft Partner Program.
