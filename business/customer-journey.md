# Customer Journey Map
## Aware
* **Trigger Event**: IT department or MSP realizes the need for SaaS application visibility in fully remote environments due to security concerns or compliance issues.
* **Friction Points**: Lack of visibility into SaaS usage, difficulty in monitoring remote employees, concern about data breaches.
* **User Emotions**: Anxiety, uncertainty, frustration.
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Educational content (blog posts, webinars) highlighting the importance of SaaS visibility, case studies of similar companies that have implemented SaaS visibility tools.
* **Metric**: Website traffic, social media engagement, lead generation (e.g., 100 new leads per quarter).

## Consider
* **Trigger Event**: IT department or MSP researches available SaaS visibility tools, reads reviews, and asks for referrals.
* **Friction Points**: Overwhelming number of options, difficulty in comparing features and pricing, concern about integration with existing infrastructure.
* **User Emotions**: Confusion, skepticism, curiosity.
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Free trials or demos, detailed comparison charts, customer testimonials, and a clear pricing page.
* **Metric**: Trial sign-ups (e.g., 20 trials per month), sales-qualified leads (e.g., 10 SQLs per quarter).

## Try
* **Trigger Event**: IT department or MSP signs up for a free trial or demo of saas-visibility-guard.
* **Friction Points**: Difficulty in setting up the tool, limited support, concern about data accuracy.
* **User Emotions**: Excitement, anticipation, uncertainty.
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Easy onboarding process, responsive support team, clear documentation, and a comprehensive user guide.
* **Metric**: Trial-to-paid conversion rate (e.g., 30% conversion rate), customer satisfaction (CSAT) score (e.g., 85% CSAT).

## Adopt
* **Trigger Event**: IT department or MSP purchases saas-visibility-guard and begins using it to monitor SaaS applications.
* **Friction Points**: Integration issues, limited customization options, concern about scalability.
* **User Emotions**: Relief, satisfaction, enthusiasm.
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Regular software updates, customizable dashboards, scalable infrastructure, and a dedicated customer success team.
* **Metric**: Customer retention rate (e.g., 90% retention rate), net promoter score (NPS) (e.g., 40 NPS).

## Expand
* **Trigger Event**: IT department or MSP realizes the value of saas-visibility-guard and wants to expand its use to other departments or teams.
* **Friction Points**: Limited budget, concern about additional complexity, difficulty in justifying ROI.
* **User Emotions**: Loyalty, advocacy, excitement.
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Flexible pricing plans, additional features and integrations, case studies and ROI analyses, and a customer advocacy program.
* **Metric**: Upsell and cross-sell rates (e.g., 20% upsell rate), customer referral rate (e.g., 15% referral rate).
